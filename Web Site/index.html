Our Mission: Save animals from kill shelters

How we plan to accomplish this: Connecting prospective pet owners with people and organizations seeking new homes for their pets (both permanent and temporary)

Facts:

  • Approximately 7.6 million companion animals enter animal shelters every year
  • Only 2.7 million of them are adopted
  • Another 2.7 million of them are euthanized

Features

  • Simple, user-friendly interface
  • Postings are sorted by location, showing you the nearest ones first
  • Connection with a third party that can adopt your pet if no one else adopts it
